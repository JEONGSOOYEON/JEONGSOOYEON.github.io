The Master and Margarita is a novel by Russian writer Mikhail Bulgakov, written in the Soviet Union between 1928 and 1940 during Stalin's regime.

A censored version was published in Moscow magazine in 19966-1967, after the writer's death. The manuscript was not published as a book until 1967, in Paris.
